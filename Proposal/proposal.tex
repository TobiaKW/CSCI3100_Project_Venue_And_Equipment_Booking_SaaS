\section{Topic: CUHK Venue and Equipment Booking SaaS}
\subsection{Planned N-1 Features}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item ActionMailer to send email notification to the user when the booking is confirmed and to the admin when there is pending approval.
    \item Sidekiq+Redis to implement Daily digest to each Department admin (e.g. “Today’s bookings in the department”, “Pending approvals”.)
    \item ActionCable to implement real-time booking status updates.
    \item Chartkick dashboard to show the usage statistics of the venues or equipments.
    %mobile display?
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Overview}
Multi-Tenant SaaS Application built with \textbf{Rails 7.2.3} with Conflict detection and Approval workflow as the Core features.

\vspace{0.5em}

\noindent\textbf{Data Models:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Department} (tenant isolation: Engineering, Arts, Medicine)
    \item \textbf{Resources} (2 types: Room or Equipment) 
    \item \textbf{Booking} (status: pending $\to$ approved/rejected/expired)
    \item \textbf{User} (student submits, admin approves)
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.5em}

\noindent\textbf{Major Pain Points and Solutions:}

\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{|p{8cm}|p{8cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Pain Points} & \textbf{Solutions} \\
    \hline
    \textbf{Fragmented booking} \newline Double booking may happen since every dept have separate Excel spreadsheets &
    \textbf{Conflict detection} \newline Single Rails database check availability in real-time \\
    \hline
    \textbf{No approval workflow} \newline Direct booking without approval may cause conflicts (e.g. proposed exam) &
    \textbf{Multi-stage approval} \newline Student books → PENDING \newline→ Admin [Approve/Reject] button \\
    \hline
    \textbf{Poor visibility} \newline No current room/equipment utilization statistics &
    \textbf{Chartkick dashboard} \newline Room usage charts + peak times + email notifications \\
    \hline
    \textbf{Manual communication} \newline Need email chains to get approval without a centralized system, may be time-consuming &
    \textbf{ActionCable + ActionMailer} \newline Admin approve → instant browser notification + confirmation email \\
    \hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
